Specifications

Driver Size:

The 40mm drivers deliver powerful sound and deep bass, providing an immersive audio experience. This size is optimized to create a balanced sound profile that enhances music playback across genres.

  • Designed for superior audio clarity
  • Produces deep, resonant bass
  • Ideal for all types of music, from classical to rock
  • Offers an expansive soundstage for better immersion

Experience music like never before with rich, immersive sound that takes your listening experience to the next level!

Frequency Response:

This range ensures that you can hear all audio frequencies clearly, from deep bass to crisp highs. With a frequency response ranging from 20Hz to 20kHz, this model captures the full spectrum of sound.

  • Ensures detailed sound reproduction
  • Highlights subtle nuances in recordings
  • Perfect for audiophiles and casual listeners alike
  • Reproduces clear dialogue for podcasts and audiobooks

Feel every note and beat with clarity and precision, elevating your audio experience to unmatched levels!

Battery Life:

Enjoy long listening sessions without the need for constant recharging, thanks to the state-of-the-art battery technology that can last up to 30 hours on a single charge.

  • Quick charging capabilities
  • Lasts through the longest playlists and commutes
  • Low battery indicator to keep you informed
  • Perfect for travel and extended use

Stay immersed in your music without interruptions, perfect for long flights, road trips, or daily use!

Weight:

Lightweight design ensures comfort during extended use, weighing only 250 grams. This makes it perfect for long listening sessions, whether you're at home, commuting, or at the gym.

  • Adjustable headband for a personalized fit
  • Padded ear cushions for all-day comfort
  • Compact design for easy portability
  • Forget you're wearing them during extended use

Immerse yourself in your favorite tracks without discomfort!
